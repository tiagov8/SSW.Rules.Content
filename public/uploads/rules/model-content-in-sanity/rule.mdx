In Sanity, content modelling is everything.

A common mistake is treating the CMS like a page builder instead of defining reusable, structured content types.

<!--endintro-->

## Why this matters

Good content models improve consistency, make content reusable across channels, and reduce frontend complexity.

They also make API usage cleaner and future redesigns much easier.

## Common uses

* Authors separated from Blog Posts
* Testimonials as reusable references instead of duplicated text
* Shared SEO objects used across multiple content types
